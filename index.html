Echoes of Rust

A rules-lite tabletop experience inspired by the dreamlike decay and surreal silhouettes of Zdzisław Beksiński. Drop this into a GitHub repository, enable Pages, and begin sharing your haunted worlds.

How to deploy

  1. Create a new GitHub repository and push these files.
  2. In Settings → Pages, choose the branch (e.g., main) and root folder.
  3. Save, then visit your Pages URL when the build finishes.

Customize your bleak frontier

  • Swap in your own art or silhouettes to set the tone for each realm.
  • Describe factions, relics, and travel rules right in the cards below.
  • Everything lives in plain HTML + CSS—no build steps, just publish.

Table prompts & mood

  • Open sessions with a brief, surreal image: distant towers, skeletal engines, rusted suns.
  • Keep stakes personal: broken oaths, lost voices, fragile keepsakes.
  • Invite players to sketch what they see—let their marks join the decay.
